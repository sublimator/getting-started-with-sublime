Welcome

Welcome to Getting Started With Sublime. We'll first do a high level pass of Sublime, taking in some of its concepts.

After that we'll have a look at basic configuration: customizing some inbuilt keyboard shortcuts and then going on to setting up your favorite font and theme.

Next we'll walk through through writing a plugin, packaging it, configuring bindings and creating a menu for it, top to bottom.

Finally we'll send you off into the real world where you can fend for yourself. We'll refer you to handy information and useful resources: informative forum posts, where to find more snippets, macros and plugins.

How to get started

  • Read this Getting Started guide and then jump in.
  • RTFM: It is written to be read in order so you'll get the most out of it that way.
  • Read the FAQ
  • Browse the WIKI
  • Check out the official references in the support section of the Sublime site
  • In between all these steps take the time to have a play around

How to finish

  • Pester the friendly folk on the forum
  • Help out with some docs for the next New Guy or Gal
