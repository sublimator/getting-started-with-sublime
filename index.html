Welcome

Welcome to Getting Started With Sublime. We'll first do a high level pass of Sublime, taking in some of its concepts. Along the way there will be some optional exercises for the types that like to get there hands dirty with something concrete. Don't worry if anything unexplained contained in these sections is beyond you. In time things will become clear so just move on and don't get distracted.

After the high level look at various subjects we'll have a look at basic configuration: customizing some inbuilt keyboard shortcuts and then going on to setting up your favorite font and theme.

Next we'll walk through through writing a simple plugin command top to bottom: packaging it, configuring key bindings and creating a menu for it.

Finally we'll send you off into the real world where you can fend for yourself. We'll refer you to handy information and useful resources: informative forum posts and where to find more snippets, macros and plugins.

How to get started

  • Read this Getting Started guide and then jump in.
  • RTFM: It is written to be read in order so you'll get the most out of it that way.
  • Read the FAQ
  • Browse the WIKI
  • Check out the official references in the support section of the Sublime site
  • In between all these steps take the time to have a play around

How to finish

  • Pester the friendly folk on the forum
  • Help out with some docs for the next New Guy or Gal
